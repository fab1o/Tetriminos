Help & Privacy Policy

Touch screen

  • Touch the game screen and hold to move the block. Drag block to satisfied location.
  • Double tap to rotate it.
  • Swipe down to drop it.

Keyboard

  • Press left (←) or right arrow key (→) to move the block.
  • Press up arrow key (↑) to rotate it.
  • Press space bar to drop the block.

Mouse

  • Click and drag the block to move it.
  • Double click to rotate it.
  • Throw the block down to drop it.

Privacy Policy

The application does not collect any personal data of users and is for the sole purpose of delivering the requested information to the users.
